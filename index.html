Nuclear Disaster Relief Incorporated

We're here to demand justice for the victims of nuclear fallout.




•Who are we?

We represent a team of concerned individuals seeking to secure compensation for those who have had their lives destroyed by the impact of poorly managed nuclear energy.


•What do we do?

For the last 15 years, we have been diligent in travelling into disaster zones and gathering evidence to allow us to file lawsuits against the companies resposible for such reprehensible tragedies as the Chernobyl and Fukushima devestations.
